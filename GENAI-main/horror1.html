The Forgotten Asylum

Story Beginning

Maya, a paranormal investigator, takes her team to the infamous Grayson Asylum, a decayed structure abandoned for decades. Local legends tell of a doctor who performed cruel experiments on patients, leaving behind restless spirits. The asylum's iron gates creak open as Maya steps inside, armed with only her camera and flashlight.

As the group explores, they encounter strange occurrences:

  • Whispers echoing in the halls.
  • Cold spots that make their breath visible.
  • A journal belonging to the head doctor, detailing experiments on "harvesting souls."

Maya finds herself separated from her team, with the unsettling feeling that someone—or something—is watching her.
