Multiple Endings

Ending 1: The Time Travel Plot

Mira learns that Dr. Hale invented a time-travel device. He used it to escape from his enemies, but he is now trapped in the past. She discovers his location and retrieves him, but the time machine’s existence remains a dangerous secret.

Ending 2: The Rival’s Revenge

Dr. Sylvia Lin sabotaged the lab and kidnapped Hale to steal his invention. Mira rescues Hale, but his invention falls into the wrong hands, creating global chaos.

The Assistant’s Betrayal

Lucas, the assistant, sold Hale’s invention to a foreign agency. Hale discovered the betrayal and tried to flee but was captured. Mira uncovers the betrayal and saves Hale, exposing an international conspiracy.
