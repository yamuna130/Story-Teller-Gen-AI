The Omega Signal

In the year 2147, humanity receives its first extraterrestrial signal, called the Omega Signal. It contains advanced mathematical codes that lead to the construction of a massive portal device. Dr. Elena Carter, an astrophysicist, spearheads the project to decipher the signal, racing against time as Earth faces ecological collapse.

When the portal activates, it reveals a shimmering wormhole. A message in the alien code warns: “Choose wisely. Only one path leads to survival.”

Elena must decide whether to:

  • Send a probe first.
  • Step into the wormhole herself.
  • Deactivate the portal entirely.

Ending 2: A Leap of Faith

This ending highlights humanity’s ingenuity and resilience, offering a hopeful but challenging future.
