Multiple Endings

Ending 1: A Risk Worth Taking

Emma confronts Aiden, learning he’s grieving the loss of his former fiancée. They bond over shared vulnerabilities, and Aiden encourages Emma to exhibit her artwork in town. Their relationship blossoms as they heal together.

Ending 2: A Heart Set Free

Emma decides to let go of Aiden and focus on her art. Her paintings, inspired by their fleeting connection, earn her recognition, and she finds inner peace, realizing she doesn’t need another person to feel complete.

Ending 3: The Mystery Unfolds

Emma confronts Aiden, discovering he is secretly trying to save the café from closure. They work together to raise funds, forming a deeper bond. In the end, they confess their love for one another.
