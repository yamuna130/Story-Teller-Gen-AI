Multiple Endings

Ending 1: The Passenger Conspiracy

Lila pieces together that the passengers are all part of a criminal smuggling ring. She outsmarts them by activating an emergency signal, leading to their capture. She later publishes an exposé on the smuggling operation.

Ending 2: The Conductor’s Secret

The conductor reveals the train is rigged with explosives as part of a sabotage plot. Lila disarms the bomb with his help, but he vanishes, leaving her to uncover a larger conspiracy tied to the blackout.

Ending 3: The Red Herring

The note was a distraction. The true danger comes from a hidden gas leak in the tunnel. Lila evacuates the passengers, saving their lives but uncovering no greater mystery, leaving her questioning the note’s origin.
