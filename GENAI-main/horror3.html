Analysis of Endings

Ending 1: Offers a bittersweet conclusion

Maya achieves peace for the spirits but loses tangible proof of her experience, giving this ending a bittersweet tone.

Ending 2: Delivers a chilling, unresolved fate

This ending leaves Maya trapped in an endless loop of haunting visions, perfect for a classic horror feel.

Ending 3: Combines action and suspense

Maya confronts the lingering soul of the doctor, narrowly escaping and leaving the asylum's horrors for the next victim, setting the stage for future hauntings.
