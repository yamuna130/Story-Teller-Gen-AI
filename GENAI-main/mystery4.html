The Vanishing of Dr. Hale

Detective Mira Cole is called to investigate the disappearance of renowned scientist Dr. Hale, who vanished from his locked lab. The lab shows no signs of forced entry, but Hale's journal reveals cryptic notes about a groundbreaking invention. Mira discovers a list of suspects:

  • Dr. Hale’s jealous assistant, Lucas.
  • Rival scientist, Dr. Sylvia Lin.
  • His estranged brother, Marcus.

As Mira investigates, she finds a hidden blueprint of a device labeled “The Gateway.”

Ending 3: The Assistant’s Betrayal

Mira’s triumph against an international conspiracy provides closure while keeping the story grounded in reality.
