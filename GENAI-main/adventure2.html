Multiple Endings

Ending 1: The True Treasure

Liam and Sophia solve the riddle, realizing it’s not material wealth they seek but knowledge. They leave behind their greed, discovering that Zephar’s treasure is an ancient library preserving humanity’s lost wisdom.

Ending 2: A Price Too High

Victor forces his way into Zephar, triggering deadly traps that collapse the kingdom’s ruins. Liam and Sophia narrowly escape but lose the map forever, ensuring no one else can exploit the treasure.

Ending 3: The Greedy Betrayal

Liam sacrifices his bond with Sophia, choosing the treasure over their friendship. He claims Zephar’s gold but finds himself cursed to guard it for eternity, as the treasure exacts a heavy toll on its seekers.
