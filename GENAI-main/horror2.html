Multiple Endings

Ending 1: The Spirits’ Revenge

Maya discovers that the spirits are not malevolent but seeking release from their torment. She performs a ritual, freeing them and destroying the asylum, though she loses her camera, erasing all evidence of their experience.

Ending 2: Trapped Forever

Maya’s curiosity triggers the asylum’s curse. She becomes trapped in an endless loop of haunting visions, her voice added to the whispers heard by the next set of explorers.

Ending 3: The Doctor’s Return

Maya uncovers the truth: the doctor’s soul lingers, feeding on fear. She confronts him but narrowly escapes, leaving the asylum standing, its horrors preserved for the next victim.
