The Midnight Train

Lila, a journalist, boards the last train of the night to escape from a citywide blackout. The train is eerily empty, except for a few passengers. As the train speeds through the night, she notices strange behavior:

  • A man clutching a briefcase and sweating profusely.
  • A woman staring blankly at her phone with no signal.
  • A conductor who seems overly nervous.

When the train abruptly halts in a dark tunnel, Lila discovers an abandoned backpack containing a note: "They’re watching. Trust no one."

Ending 2: The Conductor’s Secret

This ending maintains suspense, expands the story’s scope, and hints at further intrigue while giving Lila an active role in saving the day.
