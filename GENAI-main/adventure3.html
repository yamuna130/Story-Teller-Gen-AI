Analysis of Endings

Ending 1: Focuses on growth and the value of knowledge over greed.

This ending emphasizes character development and the importance of knowledge, suggesting that true treasure lies in wisdom and learning rather than material wealth.

Ending 2: Adds suspense and closure, preserving the mystery of Zephar.

This ending introduces elements of suspense and ensures that the mystery of Zephar remains intact, providing a satisfying yet open-ended conclusion.

Ending 3: Delivers a cautionary tale about greed and the cost of betrayal.

This ending serves as a cautionary tale, highlighting the destructive nature of greed and betrayal and the heavy consequences that follow.
