Multiple Endings

Ending 1: The Heir to the Forest (Happy Ending)

Inside the tree, Lyra finds an ancient spirit who reveals that she is the chosen guardian of the forest. The locket was left by her ancestors, and her destiny is to protect the balance of nature. With newfound powers, Lyra returns to Aldermere, ensuring peace between the village and the forest.

Ending 2: The Curse of Curiosity (Tragic Ending)

The room is a trap set by a malevolent force. The door slams shut behind Lyra, and the locket fuses to her hand, draining her life force to sustain the forest's magic. Lyra becomes one with the forest, her essence trapped forever as part of the ancient oak.
